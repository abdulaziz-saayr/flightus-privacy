Frequently asked questions

How do I add a flight?

Open the app, tap the add (+) button, enter your flight number and departure date, then tap Find flight.

Do I need an account?

No. Flightus does not require an account or sign-in. Your saved flights are stored on your device.

Why is a flight not found or missing details?

Flight information comes from third-party data sources and may occasionally be unavailable or delayed. Double-check the flight number and date, and try again. If it still doesn't appear, email us and we'll look into it.

Is my data private?

Yes. Flightus does not require an account, does not track you, and stores your flights on your device. You can read the full details in our Privacy Policy.

How do I delete my data?

Because your flights are stored only on your device, deleting the app removes them.
