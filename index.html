Privacy Policy for Flightus

Last updated: 27 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Flightus mobile app ("Flightus", "we", "us", or "the app") handles information. We built Flightus to respect your privacy. We do not require an account, we do not track you, and we do not sell your data. Please read this policy to understand our practices.

Information we collect

Flightus does not require you to create an account or sign in, and we do not ask for your name, email address, or phone number to use the app.

Flight information you enter. When you add a flight (such as a flight number, route, or date), this information is stored locally on your own device. It is not uploaded to or stored on any server owned by us.

Device location. Flightus does not request or use your device's GPS location. Map and weather information is based on the airports associated with the flights you enter, not on your physical location.

Analytics and tracking. Flightus does not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking tools. We do not collect usage statistics, and no tracking technologies are built into the app.

Third-party services

To provide its features, the app sends requests to a number of third-party services, including AviationStack, Unsplash, and Open-Meteo, and it can open the Apple App Store. When the app contacts these services over the internet, those providers may automatically receive standard technical information that is part of any internet request, such as your device's IP address. We do not control how these third parties handle that information, and we encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.

Flightus does not share the flights you enter with these services beyond what is needed to fetch the requested information (for example, an airport or city name).

How your information is stored

The flights and preferences you save are kept only on your device using your device's local storage. If you delete the app, this locally stored information is removed along with it.

Notifications

Flightus may send you local notifications (for example, reminders about an upcoming flight). These are generated on your device and do not involve collecting personal information.

Children's privacy

Flightus is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.
